Self Composition

I composed this reel myself last year after being inspired by a news article I read. My brother came across a report on the internet about a donkey which had to be rescued after falling into a well. The name [Donkey in the Well], I thought, would make an interesting tune title so I wrote down a few notes and began to work on the tune, this being the end product. I hope you like it.

PS: I post my tunes, not only for feedback, but also to see if they bear extreme similarities which already existing tunes, where by I decide to scrap them.
